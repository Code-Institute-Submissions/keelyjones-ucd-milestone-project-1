Reverend Blue

Reverend are a London based band that plays original music inspired by blues, funk, soul, and rock. Established in November 2018, Reverend Blue have hit the ground running.

In the very short time since their formation, Reverend Blue have created a cult following with talent flowing to the brim.

George Lewis, Oxford Daily Info

Meet the Band

Henry Kimber, Guitar and Vocals

Multi-talented Kimber is the face and voice of this soulful group. As I'm sure Reverend Blue's fans can hear, Kimber was trained as a classical singer, before coming to the guitar as an aside. Kimber first met Tate when they sang together in Christ Church Cathedral, Oxford and the pair have been making music together ever since.

Tom Bergquist, Guitar

When he was studying Archaeology at Durham University, Bergquist first met bassist Tate and they started making music together. A London boy through and through, Bergquist has been gigging from a young age in venues in and around the capital. Bergquist has always been a lover of funk, and you can hear that passion rippling through Reverend Blue's tracks.

Alex Burch, Drums

Burch, Reverend Blue's resident drummer loves all kinds of alternative and less well know music, and loves discovering new talent. Burch started playing drums at Lancing College, where he first met fellow band-mate Bergquist.

Dan Tate, Bass Guitar

Tate is the force that brought this group of talented musicians together. Like the rest of the band, Tate didn't start his musical career on the bass guitar. Tate started playing the violin at a young age, and now works as a professional bass-baritone singer, conductor and music teacher.
