A newly formed London based band that plays original music inspired by blues, funk, soul, and rock.

Artists We Also Like .....
BB King - Snarky Puppy - Jimi Hendrix - Miles Davis - James Brown - The Meters - Vulfpeck - Louis Cole - Ramnastax - Tom Misch - Freddie King - Marcus King Band - Cory Henry and the Funk Apostles - John Coltrane

We defy being pigeonholed, and celebrate the fact that each of our songs tells a story and in a style all its own. Come to hear us live and you'll find songs of universal resonance that are accessible, yet never dumbed down.

The band was born of a need to write and perform original music across multiple genres. We've all come from different musical backgrounds and training, from classical to hard rock; catch any of our live performances and you'll find yourself transported seamlessly from folk, to funk, to blues, to jazz, to ballads, to you name it.

We hope you enjoy listening to some of our tracks here on our site...
We would love to hear from you, and can't wait to see you at one of our gigs soon!
