Reverend are a London based band that plays original music inspired by blues, funk, soul, and rock.

Artists We Also Like ..... BB King - Snarky Puppy - Jimi Hendrix - Miles Davis - James Brown - The Meters - Vulfpeck - Louis Cole - Ramnastax - Tom Misch - Freddie King - Marcus King Band - Cory Henry and the Funk Apostles - John Coltrane....

We would love to hear from you, and hope to see you at one of our gigs soon!
